Our Story

Coffee Break is a specialized coffee exporter based in the heart of Kerinci, Sumatra. We have built our reputation on delivering exceptional quality coffee beans to international markets, maintaining the highest standards in every step of our process.

Our coffee originates from the fertile volcanic soils of Kerinci highlands, where traditional farming methods meet modern quality control standards. We work directly with local farmers to ensure sustainable practices and premium quality.
